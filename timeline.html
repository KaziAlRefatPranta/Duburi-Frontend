  • Year 2018

    Singapore AUV Challenge 2018


    “BRACU Duburi” is the first autonomous underwater vehicle of Bangladesh made by students of BRAC University. “Duburi” team was successful to secure 7th position in SAUVC (Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge) 2018. Brac University students have made it to the finals of an international underwater rover competition with a low-budget and self-made autonomous underwater vehicle ‘Duburi’. The team went through to final 15 of Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Competition (SAUVC), organised by IEEE and OES (Oceanic Engineering Society), at Singapore. ‘BracU Duburi’ is believed to be Bangladesh’s first-ever autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) to have participated in an international competition abroad. It underwent a primary selection round among a pool of 46 rovers competing from 13 countries.
